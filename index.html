About Signal48
How we collect, score, and maintain the data you see on the platform.

What Signal48 tracks

Signal48 monitors corporate expansion announcements globally — new manufacturing plants, logistics hubs, R&D centres, data centres, office expansions, and new market entries. We also track Global Capability Centre (GCC) openings across India's major tech hubs.

GCC Watch

GCC Watch is a dedicated tracker for Global Capability Centre announcements in India — covering new GCC establishments, expansions, and job creation across Hyderabad, Pune, Bengaluru, Chennai, and other emerging hubs. Data is sourced from press releases, Economic Times, Times of India, and company IR pages.

Data sources

📰Press releases & company IR
🏛Government investment agencies
📡Business intelligence feeds
🗞Economic Times / Times of India
📊SEC & regulatory filings
🌐Regional economic development boards

How often is data updated?

The dataset is reviewed and updated 48 Hours. New signals are added every Monday morning (UTC).
